Anapnea Birthday Compilation Album

By firebrandboy | Published: January 30, 2011


Firebrand Boy - Living Lost

My tracks Living Lost and Tycho Bear feature on Anapnea’s new compilation album. Grab it here today!

Anapnea aims to create a community with a spirit of productivity, learning and freedom, to give you a breath of fresh air. We maintain an open Gentoo Linux shell network that is accessed daily by hundreds of ethical hackers, developers, designers and geeks around the world. Our network is prepared with a full development environment, network utilities, webspace and secure email, games and more. We work on exciting projects and strive for progress through integrity.

Additionally, I did some very light mastering for the compilation. Nothing too heavy handed; some light topping, tailing and EQing.
