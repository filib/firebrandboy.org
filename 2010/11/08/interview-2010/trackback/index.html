Interview (2010)

By firebrandboy | Published: November 8, 2010

“I’d been working on a classical guitar and off-kilter Celtic guitar set for some time before going in a completely different direction [with] the whole chipmusic thing,” he says. “In 2004 I found the Japanese VGM and chiptune news site VORC. I remember my mind grinding to a halt when trying to comprehend the enormity of the idea of making music on videogame systems and in particular on the Nintendo Game Boy.” Firebrand Boy via On the Radar
